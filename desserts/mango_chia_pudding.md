# Mango chia pudding
## Ingredients
* 1 large mango
* 40 g chia seeds
* 200 ml coconut milk
* 1 tbsp maple syrup
* Salt, optional
  
## Instructions
Peel and core the mango, and puree half of it and cube the other half. 
Combine mango puree, cubed mango, chia seeds, coconut milk, maple syrup, and salt to taste.
Stir thoroughly. Let sit in fridge for 15 minutes, stir again. Let sit in fridge for 15 more minutes.
Can be enjoyed, but the longer you wait, the more flavour. Keeps in fridge for up to 5 days.
